Bear

Bears have an incredible sense of smell, even better than that of dogs.

  • Scientific Name: Ursidae
  • Average Length: 1.5 to 3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 20 to 25 years
  • Habitat: Forests, mountains, tundra

Bears are large mammals found in a variety of habitats around the world. They are known for their strength, powerful claws, and ability to stand on their hind legs. Bears are omnivores and have a varied diet, including berries, fish, and small mammals. They are solitary animals, except for mothers with cubs or during mating season.
